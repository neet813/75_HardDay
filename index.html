    IdeasFood & workout inspiration

    🥑Avocado Egg BowlEasy

    Halved avocado topped with a fried egg, cherry tomatoes, and a pinch of chili flakes. High protein, healthy fats, zero processed carbs.

    🐔Grilled Chicken & GreensEasy

    Marinate chicken breast in lemon, garlic, and herbs. Grill 6 min each side. Serve over spinach and cucumber. Classic clean meal.

    🥣Overnight OatsEasy

    Rolled oats + Greek yogurt + almond milk + chia seeds. Refrigerate overnight. Add berries in the morning. Prep once, eat twice.

    🥩Steak & Sweet PotatoMedium

    Lean sirloin with roasted sweet potato wedges and steamed broccoli. Iron-rich, filling, and fits any diet that allows meat.

    🍱Meal Prep BowlMedium

    Brown rice, roasted chickpeas, cucumber, red onion, feta, and tahini drizzle. Prep 4 at once. Stays fresh 4 days in the fridge.

    🥤Green Protein SmoothieEasy

    Spinach, banana, protein powder, almond milk, and a tbsp of peanut butter. Blend 30 seconds. 35g protein, counts toward water goal.

    🍳Turkey Stir FryMedium

    Ground turkey with bell peppers, zucchini, soy sauce (or coconut aminos), and garlic over cauliflower rice. Under 30 minutes.

    💪AMRAP BodyweightMedium

    45 min AMRAP: 10 push-ups, 15 squats, 20 jumping jacks, 10 lunges each leg, 30s plank. Repeat as many rounds as possible. No equipment.

    🧘Yoga FlowEasy

    45-min vinyasa flow: sun salutations, warrior sequences, hip openers, and a 5-min savasana. Counts as workout 1 on recovery days.

    🪜Stair IntervalsHard

    If you have stairs: sprint up, walk down. 30 rounds = approx 45 min. Add a weighted backpack to increase intensity.

    🏋️Dumbbell Full BodyMedium

    4 rounds: 12 goblet squats, 10 dumbbell rows each arm, 12 shoulder press, 15 Romanian deadlifts, 12 chest press. 90s rest between rounds.

    🔥HIIT TabataHard

    20s on / 10s off x 8 rounds per exercise: burpees, mountain climbers, jump squats, high knees. 4 exercises = 16 min core. Warm up 10 min first.

    🤸Core & StretchEasy

    25 min core work (planks, dead bugs, bicycle crunches, hollow holds) + 20 min full-body stretching. Great as second workout.

    🏃45-min RunMedium

    Easy pace: conversational speed, heart rate 130–150. Counts as your outdoor workout. Go first thing in the morning to lock it in.

    🚶Power WalkEasy

    Brisk 45-min walk at a pace that elevates heart rate. Arm swing matters. Excellent low-impact outdoor option on rest days from running.

    🚴Outdoor CyclingMedium

    45–60 min bike ride with at least 20 min of sustained effort zones. Hill sprints count as intensity. Hydrate before, during, and after.

    🏔️Hill SprintsHard

    Find a hill. Sprint up, walk down. 15–20 reps over 45 min. One of the most effective fat-burning, leg-building workouts you can do outside.

    ⛹️Outdoor CircuitMedium

    Park workout: bench push-ups, step-ups, pull-ups on bars, sprint 50m, bear crawl back. 5 rounds, 45 min. Use what the park gives you.

    🏊Open Water SwimHard

    45 min of swimming laps or open water. Full-body, low joint impact, and mentally challenging. Pair with a run on strong days.
