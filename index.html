75 Hard

Mental toughness · 75 days

Complete every rule, every day for 75 days straight. Miss one — even by a minute — and you restart at Day 1.

  • Two 45-min workouts (one must be outdoors)
  • Follow a diet — no cheat meals, no alcohol
  • Drink 1 gallon (16 glasses) of water
  • Read 10 pages of non-fiction
  • Take a daily progress photo

Note: a "day" here is exactly 24 hours from when you start — not calendar midnight.
